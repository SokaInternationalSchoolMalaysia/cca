Football

Develop skills, teamwork, and a love for the game.

Get to know Football

Football ECA provides students with the opportunity to learn and enjoy the sport of football while developing important physical and teamwork skills.

Students practise football fundamentals through guided training sessions, skill drills, and team activities. The programme focuses on building a strong foundation in the sport while encouraging teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship.

Learning Objectives

  • Build a strong interest and understanding of football as a sport.
  • Develop the fundamental skills required in football.
  • Encourage fair play, teamwork, and camaraderie among players.
  • Maintain positive team morale and foster cooperation during training and matches.

Suitable Students & Why You Should Join

The Football ECA is suitable for beginner and novice players from all genders who are interested in learning the basics of football.

Students who are eager to learn the sport should take this opportunity to practise and develop their foundational skills. Mastering the basics of football takes time and consistent practice, and this programme provides the environment to do so.

For students who want to push their limits in football, improving their ball technique is an important step before progressing to more advanced gameplay strategies and tactics.

What skills will you gain?

  • Communication skills during team play
  • Improved cardiovascular fitness and stamina
  • Ball control and football techniques
  • Teamwork and cooperation
  • Sportsmanship and discipline

Activities we do

1. First Touch Training
Students practise controlling the ball effectively when receiving passes.

2. Ball Mastery Drills
Players perform footwork exercises to improve control and confidence with the ball.

3. Striking the Ball Practice
Students learn how to pass, shoot, and strike the ball with proper technique.

4. 1 vs 1 Skill Development
Players practise attacking and defending situations to develop decision-making and confidence during matches.

5. Team Training Games
Students participate in small-sided games to apply their skills and improve teamwork.
