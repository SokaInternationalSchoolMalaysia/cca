Get to know The Soka Melody Makers

The Soka Melody Makers is a mixed youth choir consisting of male and female students singing together in harmony across different voice parts such as soprano, alto, tenor, and bass.

Students learn to sing in a group while developing proper vocal technique, harmony, and stage presence. The choir performs a variety of music, including pop, contemporary, cultural, and choral pieces. Members also take part in school events, concerts, competitions, and community programmes.

Objectives

  • Develop students’ vocal and musical abilities.
  • Build teamwork, discipline, responsibility, and commitment.
  • Enhance confidence and performance skills.
  • Provide exposure to different music styles and cultural experiences.
  • Support emotional wellbeing and self-expression.

Suitable Students & Why You Should Join

Suitable for students who enjoy singing, even if they have no prior training. It is also ideal for students who enjoy teamwork and collaborative activities, or those who want to build confidence and stage presence.

The choir welcomes students who are curious about music but may not play instruments, as well as those who want to grow into disciplined, responsible, and respectful individuals.

Joining the choir is beginner-friendly and provides opportunities to perform and represent the school. Through regular practice, students develop discipline, responsibility, and commitment while improving their confidence and social skills. The choir also supports emotional wellbeing and encourages healthy self-expression.

What skills will you gain?

  • Vocal techniques such as breathing, tone, and control
  • Harmony and ensemble singing
  • Rhythm and pitch accuracy
  • Musicianship and musical understanding
  • Confidence and stage presence
  • Emotional awareness and self-expression
  • Teamwork, cooperation, and communication
  • Leadership opportunities such as section leaders or student mentors

Activities we do

1. Choir Rehearsals
Students practise vocal warm-ups, breathing techniques, and group singing to develop strong vocal foundations and harmony.

2. Ensemble Singing Practice
Members learn to sing different voice parts such as soprano, alto, tenor, and bass while blending their voices as a team.

3. Performance Preparation
Students prepare songs for school events, concerts, and community performances while developing stage confidence and expression.

4. Music Exploration
The choir explores different musical styles including pop, contemporary, cultural, and choral pieces.

5. Team-building Activities
Choir members participate in group activities that strengthen teamwork, friendship, and cooperation.

Singing in The Soka Melody Makers is about more than just music — it’s about making friends, sharing moments, and creating harmony together. Every rehearsal brings laughter, energy, and the excitement of turning voices into something beautiful. Here, music is meaningful, hearts are connected, and the fun comes naturally with every note!
