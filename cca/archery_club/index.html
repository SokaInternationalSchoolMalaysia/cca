Archery Club

Learn precision, focus, and self-discipline through archery.

Get to know Z2X Archery Club

Z2X Archery at Soka International School provides students with a safe and structured environment to learn and practise archery.

Archery is a precision sport that emphasizes proper technique, concentration, and self-discipline. Students are guided in fundamentals of shooting, equipment handling, safety rules, and sportsmanship.

Activity Day, Time, Venue & Advisor

Day: Every Thursday
Time: 4:30 PM – 5:30 PM
Venue: Archery Range / School Gym (as designated)
Advisor: School Archery Instructor

Goals of the Club

  • Introduce students to archery fundamentals safely and enjoyably.
  • Develop strong technique and safety awareness.
  • Encourage personal growth, confidence, and perseverance.
  • Promote healthy extracurricular involvement and lifelong interest in sports.
  • Foster teamwork, respect, and positive values among students.

Suitable Students & Why You Should Join

Archery Club welcomes students of all skill levels, from beginners to those wishing to improve their skills.

Students who join will benefit from learning focus, discipline, and self-control while enjoying a calm and structured sport. It is ideal for those who enjoy precision, concentration, and developing confidence through practice.

What skills will you gain?

  • Improved focus, concentration, and patience
  • Better hand-eye coordination and posture
  • Self-control, discipline, and confidence
  • Stress relief and mental calm through controlled practice
  • Responsibility, respect for rules, and safety awareness

Activities we do

1. Shooting Practice
Students practise shooting at targets under guidance, focusing on accuracy and technique.

2. Equipment Handling & Safety
Learning to handle bows, arrows, and protective gear safely while following archery rules.

3. Skill Development Drills
Exercises designed to improve aim, posture, hand-eye coordination, and consistency.

4. Friendly Competitions & Challenges
Optional practice matches or challenges to encourage concentration, goal-setting, and friendly competition.
