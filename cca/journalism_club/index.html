Get to know Journalism Club

The Journalism Club provides a dynamic platform for students to develop strong writing, research, and communication skills.

Members explore storytelling, reporting, and multimedia content creation while learning to approach school and community issues responsibly and ethically. The club encourages creativity, teamwork, and leadership through hands-on journalism activities.

Learning Objectives / Goals

  • Develop strong writing and communication skills
  • Strengthen critical thinking and research abilities
  • Promote collaboration and leadership
  • Expand awareness of school and community issues
  • Prepare students for real-world communication challenges

Suitable Students & Why You Should Join

The Journalism Club is ideal for students interested in writing, storytelling, and current events.

It welcomes curious students who enjoy research, asking questions, and exploring new perspectives. Those who are collaborative, responsible, and committed to meeting deadlines will thrive, while also gaining experience in photography, design, and multimedia content.

What skills will you gain?

  • Writing and communication skills
  • Research and investigation skills
  • Critical thinking and analysis
  • Photography and multimedia skills

Activities we do

1. Article & Feature Writing
Students research and write articles for school publications, focusing on accuracy and storytelling.

2. Interviews & Reporting
Conduct interviews with students, staff, and community members to report on events or issues.

3. Multimedia Projects
Learn photography, graphic design, and video production to complement written content.

4. Editorial Meetings
Collaborate with peers to plan content, review drafts, and provide constructive feedback.

Future Opportunities

Participation can lead to careers in journalism, media, communications, and publishing. Students will gain confidence, public speaking skills, and leadership experience, along with enhanced writing, research, and analytical abilities useful for academics and beyond.
