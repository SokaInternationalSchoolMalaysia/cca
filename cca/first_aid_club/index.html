First Aid Club

Learn life-saving skills and emergency response.

Get to know First Aid Club

First Aid Club is an activity that teaches students basic first aid and Basic Life Support (BLS).

The club aims to help students learn how to respond calmly and correctly during emergencies while increasing awareness of health and safety. Through guided lessons and practical practice, students develop essential life-saving knowledge and skills.

What does students do?

Each session is one hour and focuses on a specific first aid topic. Students learn through explanations, demonstrations, and practical activities.

Practical sessions are conducted depending on the topic so that students can practise the techniques and gain hands-on experience.

Suitable Students & Why You Should Join

First Aid Club is suitable for students who are interested in learning life-saving skills and understanding how to respond during emergencies.

Students should join to gain useful knowledge, build confidence, and develop the ability to help others when someone is injured or needs medical assistance.

What skills will you gain?

  • Basic Life Support (BLS)
  • Basic first aid skills
  • Emergency response awareness
  • Confidence in helping others during emergencies

Activities we do

1. First Aid Lessons
Students learn important first aid knowledge such as how to respond to common injuries and emergencies.

2. Basic Life Support Training
Members learn the basic steps of Basic Life Support, including how to respond when someone is unconscious.

3. Demonstrations and Practice
Teachers demonstrate correct techniques and students practise the skills during guided sessions.

4. Emergency Scenario Activities
Students participate in simple scenario-based activities to practise responding calmly and correctly in emergency situations.
