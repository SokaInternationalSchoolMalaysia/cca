Badminton Club

Develop skills, fitness, and teamwork through badminton.

Get to know Badminton Club

Badminton Club encourages students to develop discipline, confidence, and a positive character while learning badminton.

The club emphasizes teamwork, sportsmanship, respect, and maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle through structured training and practice.

Activity Day, Time, Venue & Advisor

Day: Monday – Friday (as per schedule)
Time: 3:30 PM – 4:30 PM (Session 1), 4:30 PM – 5:30 PM (Session 2)
Venue: Multi-Purpose Hall (MPH Hall)
Advisor: Teacher Pinky (Sport Coordinator), Teacher Lena (Student Affairs)

Learning Objectives

  • Develop proper badminton technique and execution of moves
  • Understand and apply proper footwork for efficient court movement
  • Learn basic and advanced game tactics and strategies
  • Improve eye-hand coordination, speed, agility, and endurance
  • Build confidence, focus, mental strength, and resilience

Suitable Students & Why You Should Join

Which Students Should Join?
Students of all skill levels, from beginners to competitive players, who enjoy staying active, learning badminton in a structured way, or preparing for school/inter-school competitions.

Why Join?
To learn badminton safely with structured coaching, improve fitness, coordination, and confidence, develop discipline, teamwork, sportsmanship, and enjoy a fun and supportive environment.

What skills will you gain?

  • Badminton Skills: Correct grip, footwork, body positioning, fundamental strokes (clear, drop, smash, net shots, defense), improved accuracy and timing
  • Physical Skills: Stamina, fitness, speed, agility, balance, coordination, strength, flexibility
  • Mental & Social Skills: Game awareness, decision-making, focus, confidence, resilience, teamwork, communication, respect

Activities we do

1. Skill Development Drills
Practice exercises to improve strokes, footwork, agility, and coordination.

2. Friendly Matches
Students participate in practice games to apply techniques and tactics.

3. Fitness & Conditioning
Exercises to enhance stamina, strength, and movement efficiency.

4. Strategy & Game Awareness
Learn basic tactics, positioning, and decision-making for matches.
